HBC Silver — Platform Overview

Complete end-to-end guide for administrators and staff

HBC Silver is a digital silver savings platform by Himalayan Bullion Company that allows customers to save towards physical silver items (coins, bars) through flexible layaway payments. Administrators manage the full customer lifecycle from registration through delivery.

Platform Key Concept A Target is a layaway plan for a specific silver item. Customers pay in instalments (by grams or NPR) at the current spot rate. When 100% of grams are paid, the target completes, an invoice is generated, and the customer can schedule collection.

Customers register directly through the HBC Silver mobile app. Registration collects identity and contact information required for KYC compliance.

Registration Steps

1
Customer opens the HBC Silver app and taps "Sign Up"
App available on iOS. Customer fills in the registration form.
2
Email OTP verification
A 6-digit verification code is sent to the customer's email. Customer must enter it within 10 minutes to verify their address.
3
Account created — status: Pending Approval
Email is verified but the account is locked pending staff review. Customer sees a "Your account is under review" message.
4

A contact record is created in Zoho Inventory with the customer's details for order management.
ℹ️
Email Configuration OTP emails are sent via SMTP. Credentials can be updated in Admin → Settings → App Settings. A static OTP 123456 is available for testing when SMTP is not configured.

Pending Approval Workflow

Staff review and approve new customer accounts

After email verification, every new customer account enters Pending Approval state. No transactions are possible until an admin approves the account. This protects the platform from fraudulent registrations.

How to Approve a Customer

1
Navigate to Customers → All Customers
In the admin sidebar under Business → Customers. Filter by Status = Pending to see only unapproved accounts.
2
Review customer details
Check the customer's name, email, phone, PAN number, and registration date. Verify identity if required by your KYC policy.
3
Click "Approve" on the customer row
The account status changes to Active. The customer can now log in and use the full app.
4
Customer number is assigned
A unique HBC-XXXXX customer number is generated and synced to Zoho as the contact reference ID.

Creating a Silver Target

How customers choose and lock a silver item for layaway

A Target is a savings plan tied to a specific silver product. The customer commits to paying the full weight of that item over time. Each customer can hold up to 3 active targets simultaneously.

Creating a Target (Customer Flow)

1
Browse available products — tap "Buy" tab
Products include silver bars (100g, 500g, 1kg) and coins. Each shows weight, making charge %, thumbnail image, and SKU.
2
Select a product and review the quote
The app shows the estimated full payment at the current silver rate: Silver Cost + Making Charge. Rate is live and updates every 60 seconds.
3
Accept terms and lock the target
Customer accepts the cancellation policy (10% penalty + buyback at spot for in-store cancellations only) and confirms.
4
Target is created — status: Active
A unique Target Number (TGT-XXXXX) is assigned. The target appears in the customer's Vault with 0% progress.
🎯
Target Pricing Formula Each payment is calculated as: Grams × Current Rate + (Grams × Rate × Making Charge%). The making charge is set per-item in the product catalogue. It can be overridden by admin for specific customers.

Making Payments

Flexible instalment payments against a target

Customers can pay any amount (minimum NPR 100) towards their active target at any time. Each payment locks the silver rate for 30 seconds to guarantee the quoted price.

Payment Flow

1
Open the target from the Vault tab
Shows current progress bar, grams paid, grams remaining, and weighted average rate.
2
Enter amount in grams or NPR
Quick-fill presets: 25%, 50%, 75%, Full remaining. The opposite field mirrors automatically. Minimum NPR 100 per payment.
3
Rate lock — 30 second countdown
The quoted rate is locked for 30 seconds. After expiry, the system auto-fetches a fresh quote preserving the entered amount.
4
Confirm payment method and pay
Available methods: Credits Wallet (instant) · ConnectIPS (bank transfer gateway) · Cash (in-store, staff-recorded).

Payment Methods

MethodHow It WorksSettlement
Credits Wallet Deduct from pre-loaded credit balance. Instant if balance ≥ payment amount. Instant
ConnectIPS Redirects to Nepal's ConnectIPS bank transfer gateway. Customer completes transfer in banking app. Near real-time
Cash Customer pays at the HBC store. Staff records the payment in the admin panel. Manual
💡
Idempotency Protection Each payment includes a client-generated idempotency key to prevent duplicate charges on network retries. Safe to retry without double-billing.

Target Completion

What happens when a target reaches 100%

When a customer's final payment brings the target to 100% of the required grams, the system automatically triggers a series of events.

Automatic Completion Sequence

1
Target status → Completed
Progress reaches 100%. The customer sees a "Ready for Collection" notification in their Vault.
2
Invoice generated locally
An invoice is automatically created in the system with line items: Silver Cost + Making Charge. Invoice number format: INV-XXXXX.
3
Invoice pushed to Zoho Inventory
A background job syncs the invoice to Zoho, creating a Sales Order and Invoice in the customer's Zoho contact record.
4
Customer can download PDF invoice
From the Invoices tab in the app, the customer can download a professional PDF invoice with the HBC logo, item details, and payment summary.
5
Collection scheduling becomes available
The customer can now choose a Sunday collection date (minimum next week) from the app.
✅
Admin View Completed targets appear in Targets → All Targets with status Completed. The admin can view the associated invoice, payment history, and trigger manual Zoho sync if needed.

Collection Scheduling

Booking a Sunday collection appointment

HBC processes silver collections every Sunday. Customers can only book a collection date for the following week or later, giving the store time to prepare the physical silver.

Customer: Schedule Collection

1
Target must be in Completed state
The "Schedule Collection" button appears once progress = 100%.
2
Select from available Sundays
The app displays the next 4 available Sundays. Earliest is at least 7 days from today.
3
Collection date confirmed
Target status shows "Collection on [Date]". Customer receives the date for in-store visit.

Admin: Manage Collections

Navigate to Targets → Collections to see all pending and upcoming collection appointments.

Silver Rates

How buy and sell rates are managed

The silver buy rate (NPR per gram) is the central pricing variable for all target payments. It is displayed in the app's home screen banner (Buy / 10g and Buy / Tola) and updates automatically.

Rate Management

MethodDetails
Auto-sync from ZohoEvery 30 minutes, the system pulls the latest rate from the silver item price in Zoho Inventory. This is the primary source.
Manual updateAdmin → Settings → Silver Rate → enter new rate → Save. Takes effect immediately for all new payment quotes.
Buyback rateAutomatically set to 96% of buy rate (4% spread). Shown to customers for portfolio value calculation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common scenarios and their solutions

A customer says they cannot log in after registering ▼
Check their account status in Customers → All Customers. If status is Pending Approval, the account has not been approved yet — click Approve. If status is Suspended, check with management before reactivating.
A target shows 100% but no invoice was generated ▼
This can happen if the background job failed. Go to Targets → Details for that target, and click Re-trigger Zoho Sync. If the invoice still doesn't appear, check Settings → Sync Dashboard for errors.
Customer paid but grams didn't update ▼
For ConnectIPS payments, the system verifies on the payment callback. If the callback was missed, go to Targets → Payments and check the payment status. If it shows Pending for more than 30 minutes, manually verify with the bank and use Admin → Add Grams with the verified amount.
How to cancel a target ▼
Target cancellations are in-person only. The customer must visit the HBC store. Staff calculates refund: Total Paid − 10% Penalty − (remaining grams × current buy rate). Use Targets → Admin Actions → Cancel Target and record the reason. A credit note can be issued if applicable.
The Zoho sync shows "Failed" for a customer ▼
Go to Settings → Zoho. Check if the OAuth token needs re-authentication (tokens expire periodically). If re-auth is complete but sync still fails, check that the Zoho Organization ID in App Settings matches the active Zoho Inventory organization.
How to update the silver rate manually ▼
Navigate to Settings → App Settings → Silver Rate. Enter the new Buy Rate per gram (NPR). Save — the rate applies immediately to all new payment quotes. Historical payments are not affected. The rate also syncs from Zoho automatically every 30 minutes if Zoho integration is active.
A customer wants to pick up their silver but missed the scheduled Sunday ▼
The customer can reschedule from the app by selecting a new Sunday from the available dates. There is no limit on how many times a collection can be rescheduled. The target remains in Completed state until delivery is confirmed.
Maximum active targets per customer ▼
Each customer can hold a maximum of 3 active targets simultaneously. This limit can be adjusted by a SuperAdmin in the application configuration. Completed and Delivered targets do not count toward this limit.
